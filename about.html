About Survivor Fantasy League

A fantasy game built for Survivor superfans.

Our Mission

To create the ultimate Survivor companion experience that deepens fan engagement through strategic gameplay, community building, and celebrating the show we all love.

What We Do

True Snake Draft

Build your team of castaways through our authentic snake draft system, just like the pros.


Weekly Active Picks

Make strategic decisions each week as the game evolves and alliances shift.


Performance Scoring

Earn points based on real Survivor gameplay, challenges, and tribal council outcomes.


Ad-Supported

Completely free to play with no cash payouts. We're supported by minimal, non-intrusive ads.

Our Team

We're a small team of passionate Survivor enthusiasts who created a rules-heavy fantasy league for over a decade and wanted to share their complex fantasy league with other fans. Our backgrounds span software development, game design, and of course, countless hours analyzing Survivor strategy. We have never missed an episode since 2000!

Monetization

Survivor Fantasy League is completely free to play and does not offer cash payouts. We're supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertisements that help us keep the servers running and the game evolving season after season.
