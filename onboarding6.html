How to Play

Game Overview

Survivor Fantasy League is like fantasy football — pick players and score points weekly.

Weekly Picks

Choose 1 active player each week. Picks lock 12 hours before the show airs.

Season End

Leaderboard resets at season end. There are no cash prizes, just bragging rights.

Snake Draft System

Players draft castaways in a snake order — 1 to N, then N to 1.
